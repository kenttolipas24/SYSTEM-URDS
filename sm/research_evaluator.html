II.    Technical Information
Rationale/Significance of Research A Microsoft-based Excel application is currently being used by the UEP System to calculate the workload equivalent of teaching personnel, where there are still certain problems and issues that need to be resolved. For example, distinct Excel application files are sent to various departments and colleges to complete independently, that is prone on encoding errors. Additionally, to protect UEP System from software piracy cases, the Microsoft Excel application must have a Microsoft license.

Activities related to research and extension are essential to educational institutions, particularly universities. They provide a substantial contribution to the creation of knowledge, the improvement of communities, and the advancement of teachers' and students' careers. However, because of their complexity and scale, controlling and overseeing these operations can be difficult. The management of these operations can be simplified and made visible with the use of an organized monitoring-system.
Objectives 1. Assess the effectiveness of the existing workload computation template.
2. Develop a user-friendly interface ensuring that the system is accessible and simple to use for all stakeholders while encoding, tracking, and reporting processes.
3. Generate complete workload and contact hours reports workload point accumulation using advanced reporting.
4. Incorporate a function that calculates the points automatically to measure the contribution of each activity.
5. Conduct an assessment using TAM/TTF on the acceptability of the Workload Computation System.
III.    Review of Literature Workload monitoring in hybrid clouds was researched by V. Naik and K. Beaty (2013), who outline the architecture and design of a cutting-edge integrated monitoring system for workload monitoring in a cloud environment. The approach outlined here enables the monitoring of a workload separated by an enterprise firewall and distributed over public and private clouds using commercially available off-the-shelf monitoring products and services.
